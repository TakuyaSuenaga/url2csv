# Competitive Intelligence Skill

## Overview

Delivers comparative insights for business, tech, or market research.

**Keywords**: analysis, competitive, research, comparison, strategy

## Instructions

1. Accept the subject (product, tool, or company) and competitors from the user
2. Identify key comparison dimensions (features, pricing, performance, UX, etc.)
3. Perform a structured comparison across those dimensions
4. Highlight strengths, weaknesses, and differentiation opportunities
5. Provide actionable strategic takeaways

## Output Format

- Comparison table (subject vs. competitors)
- SWOT-style strengths/weaknesses per competitor
- Key strategic takeaways and opportunities

## Constraints

- Avoid bias toward any particular product
- Focus on actionable insights, not just facts
- Clearly note when information may be outdated or unverified
