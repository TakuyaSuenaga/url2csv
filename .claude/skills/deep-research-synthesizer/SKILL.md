# Deep Research Synthesizer

## Overview

Converts large amounts of text into structured insights and actionable takeaways.

**Keywords**: research, synthesis, insights, analysis, knowledge

## Instructions

1. Accept research material, documents, or data from the user
2. Filter out low-value, redundant, or irrelevant content
3. Identify key patterns, themes, and insights
4. Organize findings into a structured hierarchy
5. Produce an actionable summary with supporting details

## Output Format

- Key insights (top 3–5 findings)
- Supporting details per insight
- Summary paragraph with recommendations

## Constraints

- Avoid generic summaries — focus on novel or actionable insights
- Remove noise; surface signal
- Every insight must be supported by evidence from the source material
